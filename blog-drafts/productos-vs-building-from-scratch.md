## When Building from Scratch Makes Sense

Choose the traditional approach when:

- **Highly specialized domains** - Medical devices, aerospace, or heavily regulated industries
- **Existing large teams** - You already have 50+ engineers with established processes
- **Legacy integration heavy** - Most work is integrating with existing proprietary systems
- **Competitive differentiation** - Your process itself is the product (dev tools, platforms)

## When ProductOS Makes Sense

Choose ProductOS when:

- **Speed matters** - First-mover advantage or tight deadlines
- **Small or scaling teams** - Startups, small businesses, or teams under 20 people
- **Standard product types** - SaaS, mobile apps, web platforms, marketplaces
- **Resource constraints** - Limited budget for full team
- **Rapid iteration** - Need to test multiple ideas quickly
- **Consistency needs** - Want unified design systems and code standards

## The Hybrid Approach

Many successful teams use ProductOS for:
- **Initial MVP** - Get to market fast with AI-generated foundation
- **Rapid prototyping** - Test ideas before committing full resources
- **Feature expansion** - Add new capabilities without growing headcount
- **Design systems** - Generate consistent UI components

Then transition to custom development for:
- Core differentiating features
- Complex integrations
- Highly specialized functionality

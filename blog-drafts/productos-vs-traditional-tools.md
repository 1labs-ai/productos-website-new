## When Traditional Tools Win

Choose traditional tools when:

- **Deep specialization needed** - Figma for brand design agencies
- **Existing workflow investments** - Team already efficient with current stack
- **Specific compliance requirements** - Certain tools have certifications
- **Large organization** - 500+ people with established processes

## When ProductOS Wins

Choose ProductOS when:

- **Speed is priority** - Need to ship fast
- **Small-medium teams** - 1-50 people
- **Tool fatigue** - Tired of managing multiple subscriptions
- **Consistency matters** - Want unified output quality
- **AI-ready** - Ready to embrace AI-assisted development
